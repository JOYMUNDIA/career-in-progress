Welcome

ABOUT US

Career in-progress is a not-for-profit organsiation aimed at assiting individuals of all walks of life, make informed career choices.

As soon as we learn how to talk, we are constantly asked the question, "What do you want to be when you grow up?". For others the answer comes easily and stays that way into their adult life, others, the answer changes as we get older. For some, there does not seem to be an answer. Career in-progress is here to assist you in making this crucial decision. Our job is not to make the decision for you but to empower you to make it yourself, through guided roadmaps, job simulations and expert advice.

Our mission is to offer a job window shopping experience to anybody in the process of making a career choice
